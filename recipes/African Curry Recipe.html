The African Curry Recipe

Description

This dish originated in Africa,(Mozambique to be exact).

It is composed of chicken,shrimp, or crabs and it goes well with tomato chutney and onion hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 1 by leaf
  • 1(14.5 ounce) can of whole peeled tomatoes,drained
  • 2 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 eighth teaspoon salt
  • 1 whole chicken,bones and skin removed,cut into pieces
  • 1 can of unsweetened coconut milk
  • one lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large heavy skillet over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the onion, garlic, and bay leaf, and saute until the onion is lightly browned.
  3. Mix tomatoes, curry powder,and slt into the skillet, and continue cooking about 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in the chicken, and cook 15 to 20 minutes until no longer pink and juices run clear.
  5. Reduce skillet heat to low.
  6. Stirring constantly, gradually blend in the coconut milk over a period of about 10minutes.
  7. Mix in lemon juice just before serving
