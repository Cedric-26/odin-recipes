Classic Hash Browns

Description

This hash brown recipe fries shredded potatoes in clarified butter for diner-style hash browns that are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside

Ingredients

  • A starchy potato,such as the russet potato. It is perfect for crispy hash browns
  • 3 tablespoons of clarified butter
  • Seasonings (Cayenne pepper, Paprika, salt, and Pepper.)

Steps

  1. Shred the potatoes into cold, stir, drain, and dry well
  2. Cook potatoes in butter, season, and continue cooking until a crust forms on the bottom
  3. Flip and/or stir until the potatoes are brown and crustly all over
  4. Now it is ready to be served
