White Almond Wedding Cake

Description

This wonderful white wedding cake consist of sour white cream which makes this cake moist, dense and delicious.

It can also be used for kids' birthdays, but most favored for large events such as weddings as it name includes

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25ounce) package white cake mix
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • Three quarter teaspoon of salt
  • 1(1/3) cup of water
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of almond extract
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 4 egg whites

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 165 degrees C
  2. Grease and flour an 11x13 inch cake pan
  3. Stir together white cake mix,flour,sugar, and salt in a large bowl until well mixed
  4. Pour in water,sour cream,oil,almond extract,vanilla, and egg whites.
  5. Beat with an electric mix on low until all the ingredients are mixed and moistened but some lumps still remain, about 4 minutes.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is a light golden brown.
  8. Toothpick must be inserted into the center of the cakes until it comes out clean ,about 25 minutes.
  9. Allow to cool before frosting.
